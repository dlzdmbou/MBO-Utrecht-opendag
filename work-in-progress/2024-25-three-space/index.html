❔ How to play:

Once the game starts you have 6 seconds. A ⏱️ timer will start running, but...

Every shot fired will delay the timer for a little bit. If you 👌 hit a target, the timer interval will speed up. If you 👎 miss a target, the interval will slow down.

✨ Score system:

Your 🤺 accuracy will be based on your 👌 hits & 👎 misses. Your ✨ score is based on your hits, and is multiplied by your accuracy.

Find the right balance between speed and accuracy to discover the perfect interval delays.

💯 Leaderboards

Six-second-shooter is a highly competitive game and features a detailed leaderboard to follow the pros in the scene of aimtrainers.
